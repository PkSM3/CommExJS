Welcome to the complex systems community explorer


Map your own socio-semantic network or explore sub-communities using the interactive menu on the top of the webpage.
The integration of job openings in maps aims at increasing the visibility of new positions or new candidates.







This directory is open to everybody working in the field of Complex Systems science and Complexity science. Map and explore communities or generate a printable version of their directory. Updated every few minutes.
You must have a recent browser (e.g. Firefox 10+ or Chrome 17+) to see the maps.


Mapping ECCS'13 participants: ECCS'13 partcipants are invited to add the community tag #eccs13 in their profile to create the ECCS'13 directory.
